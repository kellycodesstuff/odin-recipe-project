Smoked Salmon And Black Garlic Dip

The Perfect Party Dip

Looking for the perfect party dip to bring a little ocean to your fiesta? Look no further. This simple yet delicious dip is sure to delight your taste buds.

This dip is easy to prepare and can be served with crackers, cucumers, toast or other veggies. For a lighter option, substitute greek yogurt!

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 8 oz Sour Cream
  • 1/2 cup Green Onion diced
  • 1 clove Black Garlic minced
  • 4 oz Smoked Salmon flaked
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions:

  1. Stir cream cheese and sour cream together in a bowl until smooth; stir in green onions and black garlic.
  2. Stir salmon and salt into cream cheese mixture just until combined; refrigerate for 1 hour. Stir once before serving to distribute flavors.
